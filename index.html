My TOP 10 Favorite Treats

Everyone loves desserts, who wouldn't? Here are some of my favorite treats of all-time. If you have not tried any of these, be sure to!!

10. Fruit

This may be controversial but fruit should be a dessert! Or at least a treat. They are refreshing and sweet, what else defines a dessert?

9. Frosting

Frosting is great to eat on its own. Who needs actual cake? It can be quite sweet but is not something I would skip out on.

8. Brownies

Chocolate heaven!! Brownies are the best for chocolate lovers. They are both fudgey and chocolatey. They are amazing treats to share with friends and family so be sure to bake them for events!

7. Ice Cream

The perfect treat for a hot, sunny day. Be sure to bring some to the beach!!

6. Cookies

The best with warm milk. They can never go wrong. Either crunchy or soft, they are both amazing!!

5. Cake

The go-to dessert for any special events! Cake is something you can never tire of!!

4. Donuts

The best breakfast someone can ever have. Great with hot coffee to create a perfect morning.

3. Churros

The ultimate snack. Both sweet and warm for a nice chilly day.

2. Mochi

An amazing treat that can be filled with whatever your heart desires!!

1. Macarons

The most expensive, but most delicious!! No matter the cost, it is always worth it. They melt in your mouth and are a must try!!
